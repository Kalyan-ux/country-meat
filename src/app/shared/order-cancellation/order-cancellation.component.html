Cancellation of Orders

Users placing scheduled orders may choose to cancel their Orders free of charge, provided the cancellation is made a minimum of 24 hours before the scheduled slot start time. Cancellation requests received after this specified time window will result in a cancellation fee of up to 100% of the order value. This is because Country Meat follows a Just-in-Time model, our birds are transported from farms to the slaughtering unit only when needed, with minimal stock kept on-site. This ensures freshness, hygiene, and free-range rearing. Late cancellations aren’t possible once the birds are allocated for your order.

In case of cancellations for the reasons attributable to Country Meat, Country Meat shall not collect any penalty from the User.

To avoid any damage claims after delivery, users are recommended to inspect the product seal before accepting the order.
